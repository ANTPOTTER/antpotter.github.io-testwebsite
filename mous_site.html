A Unique Rainforest Chalet Experience Awaits

The Mouses House Retreat Springbrook is a hidden gem in the Gold Coast hinterland open to all guests - Romantic couples, families with children or a small group of friends wanting a special getaway. Our rainforest is truly enchanted and within an hour of arriving you will feel any stress disappear, replaced with peace, tranquillity & a feeling of being at one with nature.

Please Note: Our reception and back office building to be replaced, subject to council approval. Decommissioning of general hot tub and sauna hut due to age.

We are excited that final plans are undergoing approval to replace our reception building with a fabulous new hotel experience for everyone. The start date is approximately early-September 2024, subject to change. Weekday construction noise around the car park area may happen from 8 a.m. to 4 p.m., depending on weather and different work requirements. We thank you dearly for your understanding and support. As more information becomes available, it will be posted here. Please contact us for further inquiries or questions.

We would be interested to know your thoughts on us building a new general hot tub and sauna hut or not? Let us know!

Wayne and Christine, owners 20/05/24

All our self-contained chalets are beautifully appointed.

Your accommodation consists of one of 14 chalets nestled throughout the World Heritage Listed National Park. The song of native birds and the haunting call of nocturnal animals will have you in awe, not to mention the beauty of the towering brush box trees and the gentle cascading streams. Our chalet highlights include full kitchen, free Wi-Fi, movies on-demand, hardwood fireplace, large spa bath, cascading streams and some with extra private outside hot tubs!
